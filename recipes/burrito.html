Breakfast Burrito with Bacon, Hashbrowns, and Cheesy Eggs

Eggs, bacon and a hash brown, all slathered in maple butter and warmly hugged in a thin pancake wrap.

Ingredients

Wrap Filling
  • 4 hashbrown patties
  • 8 strips bacon
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 4 tablespoons maple butter
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
Pancake Wraps
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoom vanilla
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk, divided
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter

Steps

Wrap Filling
  1. Preheat oven to 425 dgree. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Place 4 hash brown patties on parchment and bake until golden, approx 20 to 25 minutes. Place strips of bacon on another sheet and bake until crispy, approx 20 minutes.
  3. While bacon and hash browns are baking, prepare the remaining ingredients for the wraps.
  4. Crack eggs into a medium-sized bowl with the remaining salt, and whisk until yolks are broken and eggs are just combined.
  5. In a large nonstick pan, melt the remaining tablespoons of butter over medium heat
  6. Pour eggs into the warm pan and turn heat down to medium-low.
  7. Cook eggs very slowly, swirling them in the pan using a wooden spoon or spatula. Eggs are done when they look silky, have come together and are still a little wet.
  8. Turn off the heat and add cheese
  9. Mix just to combine
  10. Set aside
Pancake Wraps
  1. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk eggs until lightly beaten.
  2. Add vanilla, salt and 1/3 cup of the milk, and stir until combined.
  3. Add flour and whisk until smooth
  4. While whisking, add the remaining milk. Batter should be thick and smooth.
  5. Heat a 10-inch pan on medium-high and coat with butter.
  6. Drop approximately 2/3 cup of batter onto pan and cook until golden. You will start to see bubbles form on the top of the dough when it is close to flip time.
  7. Flip over and repeat until golden
  8. Keep pancakes wamr on a plate until ready to assemble
  9. to assemble wraps: Spread maple butter in the middle of each pancake and place a hash brown on top.
  10. Place a quarter of the scrabled eggs on each hash brown
  11. Layer bacon over the eggs and drizzle with maple syrup
  12. secure wraos in parchment until serving. They will keep up to a day in the fridge.
  13. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving
