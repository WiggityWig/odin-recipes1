Classic Manhattan Cocktail

Whiskey mixed with sweet vermouth and a dash of aromatic bitters

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 2 Parts Whiskey
  • 1 part sweet vermouth
  • 1 to 2 dashes bitters, such as Angostura
  • Orange peel
  • Real Marashcino Cherries

Directions

  1. Place ice in cocktail shaker.
  2. Add the whiskey, vermouth and bitters.
  3. Rub the orange peel around the rim of the cocktail glass
  4. Strain the drink into the glass
  5. Add 1 to 2 Real Maraschino Cherries and enjoy!
